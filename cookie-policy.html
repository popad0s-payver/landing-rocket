Cookie Policy

How TechSphere uses cookies and how you can control them

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember your preferences, enhance performance, and deliver relevant content.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required for basic site functionality, security, and form submissions.
  • Performance: Collect anonymous analytics to improve speed, navigation, and content quality.
  • Functionality: Remember your language, preferences, and form details to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing: Used sparingly to understand campaign performance; no sensitive personal data is sold.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics, performance monitoring, or payment processing. These partners are contractually required to safeguard data and may set their own cookies to operate.

How to Manage Cookies

  • You can accept or reject non-essential cookies through our banner when you first visit.
  • Adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies; doing so may impact site functionality.
  • Opt out of analytics by using your browser’s “Do Not Track” setting where available.

Retention

Cookies remain on your device for set durations (from session-only to 12 months) unless you delete them manually. We review and refresh cookie lifespans regularly.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be highlighted on this page along with the updated date.
