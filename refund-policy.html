Refund Policy

How cancellations, credits, and refunds are handled

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

General Cancellation Rules

  • You can cancel at any time from your account or by contacting support.
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period; premium access continues until then.
  • We do not issue refunds for partial months or unused time after a renewal has processed.

Refund Eligibility

  • Duplicate or accidental charges caused by platform error.
  • Service unavailability lasting more than 48 consecutive hours within the billing period.
  • Billing mistakes such as being charged for a plan you did not authorize or receive.
  • Requests made within 14 days of the charge date with reasonable evidence of the issue.

Not Eligible for Refunds

  • Change-of-mind cancellations after premium content has been accessed in the billed period.
  • Charges older than 60 days.
  • Bank fees, currency conversion charges, or fees set by your payment provider.

How to Request a Refund

  • Email support@techsphere.com with the subject line “Refund Request”.
  • Include your name, the email used for the subscription, transaction date, and a short description of the issue.
  • We aim to review requests within 3 business days and will ask for any additional details if needed.

Processing Timelines

  • Approved refunds are issued back to the original payment method.
  • Most banks post credits within 5–10 business days after we process them.
  • We will confirm approval or rejection in writing.
