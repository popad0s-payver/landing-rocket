About Yash Sharma

Electronics Engineer, Technology Innovator, and Industry Pioneer

Meet the Visionary

Yash Sharma is a passionate electronics engineer with over 8 years of experience in cutting-edge technology development. Based in New Delhi, he has become a recognized voice in the Asian tech community.

Professional Journey

Started as a junior electronics engineer and quickly rose through the ranks, leading major IoT projects for Fortune 500 companies and startups alike. His innovative approach has earned him recognition across the industry.

Educational Background

Master's in Electronics Engineering

Graduated with distinction from Delhi Technological University, specializing in embedded systems and digital signal processing. Thesis focused on IoT security protocols.

Continuous Learning

Holds multiple industry certifications in AI/ML, cloud computing, and cybersecurity. Regular participant in advanced workshops and international tech conferences.

Research Publications

Author of 15+ peer-reviewed papers in international journals on topics ranging from IoT security to AI-powered embedded systems.

Core Expertise

IoT & Connected Devices

Expert in designing and implementing IoT solutions for smart cities, industrial automation, and consumer electronics. Specialized in low-power wireless protocols and edge computing.

Embedded Systems Design

Proficient in ARM Cortex processors, real-time operating systems, and hardware-software integration. Experience with automotive, medical, and aerospace applications.

AI Hardware Acceleration

Leading expert in AI chip design, neural processing units, and edge AI implementation. Focus on optimizing machine learning algorithms for resource-constrained devices.

Professional Achievements

Industry Recognition

Winner of the "Young Innovator Award" at Tech Summit Asia 2025. Featured as one of the "Top 40 Under 40" tech leaders by Electronics Weekly magazine.

Patent Portfolio

Holds 8 patents in IoT security, energy-efficient processors, and wireless communication protocols. Several more patents pending in AI hardware optimization.

Startup Success

Co-founded two successful tech startups, including an IoT security company that was acquired by a major multinational corporation in 2022.

Current Projects & Initiatives

Smart City Initiative

Leading a team developing next-generation smart traffic management systems for Delhi Municipal Corporation, expected to reduce traffic congestion by 30%.

Education & Mentoring

Active mentor at TechStars and other accelerator programs. Guest lecturer at IIT Delhi and DTU, inspiring the next generation of engineers.

Open Source Contributions

Maintains several popular open-source libraries for IoT development, with over 50,000 downloads combined. Regular contributor to major embedded systems projects.
