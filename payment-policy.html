Payment Policy

How we bill, secure, and manage TechSphere subscriptions

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Billing Overview

  • All subscription fees are quoted in MYR (RM) and include applicable taxes unless stated otherwise.
  • Plans are billed monthly in advance starting on the activation or upgrade date.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically until you cancel; cancellations stop future charges and take effect at the end of the current cycle.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay, Amex where available).
  • Secure invoicing and bank transfer options for enterprise clients upon request.
  • All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant third-party processors; we do not store raw card numbers.

Invoices and Receipts

  • Receipts are emailed to the billing contact for every successful payment.
  • Invoices display plan details, billing period start/end dates, tax amounts, and currency.
  • Update billing contacts or company details by emailing support@techsphere.com before the next renewal cycle.

Security and Fraud Prevention

  • All checkout pages are protected with SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Card information is tokenized by our payment processor; TechSphere only stores the last four digits and expiry for reference.
  • We monitor for unusual activity and may temporarily suspend access while verifying account ownership.

Failed or Disputed Payments

  • We automatically retry declined payments; you will be notified to update your details.
  • Access to premium content may be paused until payment is completed.
  • For disputes or chargebacks, contact us first at support@techsphere.com so we can investigate and resolve quickly.

Currency and Bank Fees

  • If your issuing bank bills in another currency, it may apply its own exchange rate and fees.
  • Some banks place temporary authorizations during verification; these typically clear automatically.
